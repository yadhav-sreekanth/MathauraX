Symmetry Challenges

🌟 What is Symmetry?

Symmetry means that if you fold a shape along a special line, both sides match perfectly. That line is called the line of symmetry.

Let’s look at the shapes below. Click on the ones that are symmetrical!

📚 Which Shapes Have Symmetry?

  • Square – ✅ Yes! It has 4 lines of symmetry.
  • Rectangle – ✅ Yes! It has 2 lines of symmetry.
  • Equilateral Triangle – ✅ Yes! It has 3 lines of symmetry.
  • Scalene Triangle – ❌ No! It has no lines of symmetry.
  • Trapezoid – ❌ No! Most trapezoids don’t have symmetry.
