Measurement

Measurement is the process of determining the size, length, or amount of something using standard units.

Types of Measurement

  • Length: meter (m), centimeter (cm), kilometer (km)
  • Mass: gram (g), kilogram (kg), milligram (mg)
  • Volume: liter (L), milliliter (mL), cubic meter (m³)
  • Time: seconds (s), minutes, hours
  • Area: square meter (m²), hectare
  • Temperature: Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F)

Unit Conversions

Convert 5 kilometers to meters:

  1. 1 kilometer = 1000 meters
  2. So, 5 km = 5 × 1000 = 5000 meters

Convert 2500 mL to liters:

  1. 1000 mL = 1 liter
  2. So, 2500 mL = 2500 ÷ 1000 = 2.5 liters

Example Problems

Find the area of a rectangle with length 8 cm and width 5 cm.

Area = length × width = 8 × 5 = 40 cm²

Convert 3.5 kg to grams:

1 kg = 1000 g → 3.5 kg = 3.5 × 1000 = 3500 g

Practice Problems

1. Convert 2.75 liters to milliliters.

2.75 × 1000 = 2750 mL

2. Find the perimeter of a square with side length 6 cm.

Perimeter = 4 × side = 4 × 6 = 24 cm

3. Convert 7200 seconds into hours.

7200 ÷ 3600 = 2 hours
